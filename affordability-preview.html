Methodology

Based on the Atlanta Fed Home Ownership Affordability Monitor. Total Cost of Ownership (TCO) = Annual Mortgage P&I + Annual Property Tax + Annual Homeowners Insurance + Annual PMI (if applicable). Affordability ratio = TCO / Median Household Income. A ratio above 30% is generally considered cost-burdened per HUD guidelines.
